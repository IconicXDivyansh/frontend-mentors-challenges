FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor is a platform that provides real-world front-end coding challenges to help developers improve their skills.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

You can sign up by visiting the Frontend Mentor website and creating an account using your email or social media accounts.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Yes, Frontend Mentor offers a variety of free challenges for developers of all skill levels.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

Absolutely! You can submit your solutions to the community for feedback and suggestions.
